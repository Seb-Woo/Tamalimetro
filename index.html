Welcome to the Tamalimeter

Let's find where the best tamales are located around us and thus enjoy a good delicacy today, let's also learn about the time it may take us and more!.

Discover a world full of flavors and tradition with our app. Here you'll find the best tamales wherever you are, made with ancient recipes and a modern twist.

Whether you're looking for classic chicken tamales, red tamales, rajas tamales, or something more adventurous, we're here to guide you in your search for the perfect tamale. Delight your palate with our culinary guide and begin your adventure today!

How does the page work?

This page works based on the user's location and the use of coordinates, the use of custom pins, and other features related to the location of tamale stands.
Explore and determine the places as you like.
You can search through the interactive map to navigate the streets of your own city, or you can even enter the exact coordinates of the location you want to reach.
This is more a matter of individual preference.
This way, you'll also be able to find out other details, such as how long it will take to get from your location to the indicated point, whether you arrive on foot, by bike, motorcycle, or even by car.
And even some other features that can help.

What is the purpose of this page/app?

This page was created to better adapt to learning how to use:
  • APIs
  • Firebase

  • Advanced JavaScript Functions
Implemented APIs:
  • Google Maps API
  • The Cat API
  • Dog API

The interactive map uses the Google Maps API along with Firebase to store favorite locations, and the Weather API to display current conditions.
